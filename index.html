OVERVIEW

⚔️
Core Battle Loop
Each round: draw → pick a card → choose Attack or Defense → reveal → winner takes the round.
🃏
Deck Building
1–9 cards per deck. Build around synergies. No card can be used twice in a row — manage cooldowns.
🎮
Game Modes
Normal battles, Draft mode (pick from a pool), and Gauntlet (endless escalating stages).
📈
Progression
Earn XP to level up. Gold funds new packs. Win cards from battles. Daily rewards keep you growing.

LEARN ALL THE RULES

🃏 DECK RULES
1 to 9 cards max
No reuse back-to-back
2-turn cooldown per card
⚔️ BATTLE FLOW
1Draw 2 cards from your pile
2Select 1 card to play
3Choose Attack (DMG) or Defense (HP)
4Bot picks its card and stat
5Both revealed — higher value wins the round
6Winner takes the loser's card

How It Works

Four steps to becoming a legend

01
📦

Open Packs

Buy packs with gold earned from battles. Each pack holds 3 cards — commons, epics, and rare legendaries hidden until you flip them.

02
🃏

Build a Deck

Drag cards into your deck rack. Up to 9 cards per deck. Mix types and rarities — diversity beats stacking.

03
⚔️

Enter Battle

Draw cards each round. Pick one, then choose Attack (DMG) or Defense (HP). Higher stat wins the round and steals the enemy card.

04
👑

Collect & Rise

Win cards from beaten enemies. Earn XP and gold. Unlock harder difficulties and push your gauntlet record.
